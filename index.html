Fukuoka Public Transport Guide

Learn how to ride Nishitetsu Buses and Fukuoka Subway safely and politely.

Nishitetsu Bus

Nishitetsu Bus is the main bus service in Fukuoka. You can board from the rear door and exit from the front.

  • Pay when you get off the bus.
  • Prepare your IC card (e.g., Nimoca, Suica, or Hayakaken) before exiting.
  • Keep quiet and avoid phone calls on the bus.
  • Give priority seats to the elderly or people with disabilities.

Fukuoka Subway

The Fukuoka City Subway connects major areas such as Tenjin, Hakata, and the Airport. It’s fast and convenient.

  • Stand behind the yellow safety line on platforms.
  • Let passengers get off before you enter the train.
  • Do not eat or drink inside the train.
  • Keep your volume low when using headphones.
