Fukuoka Public Transport Guide

Learn how to take the transportation.

Nishitetsu Bus


Nishitetsu Bus is the main bus service in Fukuoka.
It's able to go to various places.
You can take a bus from the middle door and exit from the front.

  • Keep quiet and mustn't use phone calls on the bus.
  • Give priority seats to the elderly or people with disabilities.
  • Warning!!:Nishitetsu bus is a lot of lines. So you should be careful.
  • you should understand about what you take bus number.
  • Pay when you get off the bus.

    • You can choose how to pay with cash, Credit cards,Transportation IC card(Nimoca,hayakaken etc.).

Fukuoka Subway

The Fukuoka City Subway connects major areas such as Tenjin, Hakata, and the Airport. It’s faster than bus.

  • Stand behind the yellow safety line on platforms.
  • Let passengers get off before you enter the train.



  • Do not eat or drink inside the train.
  • Keep your volume low when using headphones and talking.
  • If you are trouble,let's ask the station staff.
  • pay when you enter the ticket gate or ticket machine.

  • You can choose how to pay with cash, Credit cards,Transportation IC card(Nimoca,hayakaken etc.). (Compatible cards are listed below)
